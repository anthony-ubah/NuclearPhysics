How To Run:

1) Unzip Ubah_7 folder.
2) Make sure folder and subfolders are included in PATH.
3) Open Ubah_7_Test and run.
4) Note that fields are not multiplied by hbarc until they are being plotted.
5) Binding Energy Test case found in Binding Energy plot submitted with values fitted annotated directly onto the graph.
6) BindE was used to calculate binding energies and for fitting.
7) BindEZ does binding energy with dependence and calculates aA.
8) Test 1 results posted in Test1 Folder.
9) Results in Ubah_7_Test were run for Oxygen 16.
